Location, age, gender

From a total of 28 countries, The Maker Report responders reside in the United States with the highest results of 26.3%, India following with 12.5% and the United Kingdom with 8.7%.

Most makers fall in the 24-28 age range (29.1%). There are also the rising talents, the 19-23-year-olds (11.7%) and the 39+ group is pushing it close to 10%.

Gender-wise, The Maker Report responders consist of mostly men, with around 80% versus 15.5% of females.
