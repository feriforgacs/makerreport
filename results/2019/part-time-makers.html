Part-time makers

50% of those working on their projects part-time, would fully focus on them only if they could make enough revenue through their products to cover their expenses.

30% would do the same if they made some revenue already and could cover their expenses in a few months.

For almost 6% percent, revenue doesn’t matter, they would go full-time on their products without a steady revenue stream.

1% wouldn’t give up on their full-time/part-time jobs to focus on their own projects.
